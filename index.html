Interesting facts about camel.

  • Camels can eat anything they find in the desert, even cacti.
  • The inside of its mouth has a thick coating, which prevents the penetration of cactus spines.
  • A camel can drink 120 liters of water in 10 minutes.

Interesting facts about Chameleon.

  • There are more than 200 species of chameleons.
  • Small, medium and large. There are chameleons for all tastes.
  • These animals use their toes and tail to move around.

Interesting facts about Pigeon.

  • The pigeon reaches around 31 cm in length.
  • They weigh about 128 grams, although there are specimens up to 170 grams.
  • Its wings measure between 37 and 45 cm.

Interesting facts about Teddy Bear.

  • Bears mainly eat meat and fish, but some also eat plants and insects.
  • Canada is home to nearly 60% of the world's polar bears.
  • The Asian black bear has larger ears than other bear species.

Interesting facts about Panda.

  • Pandas can spend up to 14 hours a day feeding.
  • A panda can defecate more than 100 times in a single day.
  • Pandas have very low digestive efficiency, as their digestive system is very similar to their carnivorous ancestors.
